Instructions


How to acess this site

The requirements for seeing anything on this site are as follow:
You must
  • 1. be viewing the site in the Eve in-game browser (IGB),
  • 2. have the site on your Trusted Sites list, and
  • 3. be playing on a character in the OI alliance
You will then be able to access data properly.

To test that everything is working correctly, go to the igb test page. If you see nothing besides the nav
menu and the header, you're missing a step. If you see a readout of your character's information, you're golden.
Also, look at the right side of the upper nav bar. If you see "Welcome, [your character's name]," you're golden. If it says "Please be
in-game and have the site Trusted," then you're doing something wrong.

What are you using the igb data for?

I check your character's name, alliance, and location for storage, verification, and storage, respectively.
All that other data I could get I don't care about.

Viewing current data overview

To see an overview of data currently in the system, visit the index page.

To see if any of the systems in the chain are hot, see the check kills page. Note that it can take a long time to load.

To see a system's status, view it's page at /sitemngr/system/[SYSTEM_NAME].
You can view a universe overview at /sitemngr/system/ to see which system have open sites.

To get information for just Nexus, click on the Output current data link on the left.

Update the database by pasting

The paste page is for quick and easy data entry into the system by means of copy-pasting
your system scanner's full data into a box and clicking a button.

The site then sees your paste, and breaks each site down and sorts into three (3) categories:

1. Exist - These ids are in the database for that system and in your paste. All's good.
2. Not found - These ids are in the database for that system, but not
in your paste. They may have despawned, so please update accordingly.
3. New - These ids are not in the database but are in your scan, so they're probably new.
The site will pick out the most information it can find, and auto-fill the most fields for you.

Edit a site individually

To edit a site, you can either click on its scanid in any system readout or the index page, or go to /sitemngr/editsite/[SCAN_ID] or /sitemngr/editwormhole/[SCAN_ID] (found on the nav menu to the left).

Adding a site/wormhole manually

Click the link on the left nav menu. Enter information appropriately, and click the submit button. The site
should read your character's name and location, and auto-fill those fields.

Graphing

A fresh graphic layout of the connections from Nexus are generated every 5 minutes. You can see the map on the homepage.

The circle colors are as such:
HS = gold
LS = purple
NS = brown
C1 = blue
C2 = green
C3 = yellow
C4 = orange
C5 = red
C6 = black
not found = black

It is important, then, to really be sure that you're typing system names as they are in-game.

Submiting bugs and feature requests

Visit the support center. This app is in Beta, so everything should work, but in case it doesn't, don't primary me.

TL;DR

Use the paste page to have the site tell you what needs to be done. Do those things.
